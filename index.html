Our Services

Select a service below for a detailed explanation.

Brickwork

At Starville Contracting, brickwork is at the core of what we do. Our team of highly skilled and experienced bricklayers brings structure, strength, and style to every project. From cavity walls and feature brickwork to retaining walls and full new-build packages, we combine traditional techniques with modern precision to deliver outstanding results. We work closely with clients, architects, and developers to ensure every detail is executed with care and craftsmanship—on time, on spec, and built to last.

Dry Lining

Our dry lining services are designed to transform interiors with speed, accuracy, and finesse. Whether it’s metal stud partitions, MF ceilings, or plasterboard systems, our experienced tradesmen install clean, straight lines and perfectly prepared surfaces—ready for decoration. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to efficiency, Starville Contracting ensures flawless finishes that enhance both residential and commercial spaces. We pride ourselves on minimal disruption, fast turnaround, and exceptional quality in every dry lining project.

Tiling

Precision and aesthetic appeal are the hallmarks of our tiling services. We supply and install a wide range of wall and floor tiles for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial areas—covering everything from ceramic and porcelain to natural stone and mosaics. Our expert tilers ensure every cut, joint, and finish is executed to the highest standard, creating surfaces that are both functional and visually striking. Whether it’s a high-end residential space or a large commercial fit-out, we bring a meticulous approach and years of expertise to every tiling job.

Painting & Decorating

Our painting and decorating team adds the final touch that brings your space to life. With an eye for colour, texture, and precision, we deliver immaculate finishes for interiors and exteriors alike. From new builds and renovations to commercial units and bespoke homes, we use only high-quality materials and professional techniques to ensure long-lasting, beautiful results. With attention to detail, clean workmanship, and a passion for perfection, Starville Contracting helps create environments that reflect your style and vision.

Starville Contracting – A Specialist in Construction Services

At Starville Contracting, construction is more than just a business—it’s a family legacy. With over 60 years of combined experience, our roots in the building trade trace back to the owner's grandfather, who laid the foundation for what would become a multi-generational commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and hard work.

Today, we carry that legacy forward with a modern approach and a highly skilled team of tradesmen who take pride in every project. Specialising in brickwork, dry lining, tiling, and painting & decorating, we provide a full range of construction services to residential developers, commercial contractors, and private clients across the UK.

What sets us apart is our hands-on ethos, our attention to detail, and our deep understanding of the construction process from the ground up. Whether it’s building solid structures with precision brickwork, transforming interiors with flawless dry lining, adding beauty and durability through expert tiling, or completing spaces with premium painting and decorating—we deliver reliable results with a personal touch.

Our clients trust us not only for our experience and expertise but for our transparency, professionalism, and commitment to doing things right the first time. At Starville Contracting, we don’t just build—we build with pride, integrity, and passion passed down through generations.
